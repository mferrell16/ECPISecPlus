⚠ Common Exam Trap
Never rank by CVSS alone. A CVSS 9.8 on an internal, air-gapped dev server with no patch in sight and EPSS 2% ranks lower than a CVSS 7.2 on an internet-facing payment API with a KEV listing and EPSS 78%.

The exam tests whether you apply context, not just score lookup.
