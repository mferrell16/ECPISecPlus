Common Exam Traps
⚠ Frequently Missed
Skipping Eradication: Going Containment → Recovery without removing the root cause means reinfection is almost guaranteed.
Skipping Lessons Learned: Recovery is not the last step. Post-incident review is required by NIST.
Detection ≠ Analysis: These are two separate phases. Detection identifies an event; Analysis determines scope, type, and impact.
Preparation is Phase 1: Not "Detection" — preparation includes building runbooks, training, and tooling before an incident.
Containment has two stages: Short-term (stop the bleeding) and long-term (stable temporary fix) both fall under Containment.
